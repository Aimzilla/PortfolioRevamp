I am a front-end developer who is enjoying learning about front-end development. I am always looking forward to my next opportunity to learn and try something new.

A little about me...

My journey into tech started with an undergraduate degree in Sociology, and a Master's degree in Business. Subsequently, I joined the Peace Corps, where I served in Namibia. While there, I became aware of just how much technology was used around the world, and realized that it could be a valuable tool with which to teach and share information.

I was in Namibia when I decided that upon my return to the U.S., I wanted to go into tech. After taking a couple of courses online and attending a coding bootcamp, I have determined that I am most interested in website development; specifically front-end development. I hope to use my new and growing skills along with my pre-existing skillset to work for non-profits and marginalized communities.
